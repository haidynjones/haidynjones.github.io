Reflection

The WordPress Portfolio assignment was one of my favorites. For this assignment, we created personal blogs or portfolios of our media careers so far. I think in the end, this site turned out the best and the most aesthetically pleasing. I really liked the color scheme. I thoguht that this site was also the most clean looking, as it wasn't busy and full of a ton of pitures or links. This is one things that can be overwhelming when I am on a website, so it was one of my goals to keep it uncluttered. I also think the simplicity of using the WordPress site helped move things along quicker. I definately think this site is a great collection of all of my mediapro work throughout college. My favorite page on this site is my work page with all of the videos I have produced for various classes.
